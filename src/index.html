FOOD HIVE RESTAURANT

where your taste buds come alive

Tasty, Scrumptious Delicacies...Everytime

That is the promise at FoodHive Restaurant. Every meal is a work of art crafted with the idea that food is an item that should first delight the senses, and then be enjoyed. Our chefs are not just cooks, they are artists, versed in their craft to create masterful culinary experiences and delicacies that are sure to leave your taste buds screaming for more with each morsel.

And that's not all, asides the promise of well cooked delicacies and treats, is the ambience at FoodHive that is not just beautiful, it is breath-taking. Everything from the lighting to the decor, from the welcome you receive at the entrance to the play area for the kids; everything is designed tastefully to be aesthetically pleasing because we believe every visit to FoodHive restaurant should be an unforgettable experience.

WHAT'S ON THE MENU?

Some of our mouthwatering offerings

Efo Riro

Delicious Efo Riro sauce prepared just the way you like it with ponmo, dried fish, and your choice of either beef, goat meat or poultry.

N3500

Palm Oil Jollof

Delicious palm oil jollof rice prepared with plenty of dried red shrimp and dried fish. You can have this with any meat product of your choice. Real yummy.

N3000

Edikanekong Soup

Scrumptious Edikanekong soup with addons. Ponmo: check, periwinkles: check, snails: check, dried fish: check... Are you getting the picture?

N7000

Sea Food Okro

Delicious sea food Okro packed with shrimps, prawns, fresh fish, and snails. You also have the option of crabs and meat products.

N3500
